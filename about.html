..Who we Are..

CocoRich is an Australian company specializing in the import and marketing of high-quality coconut-based products sourced from Sri Lanka, a pioneering Coconut-Based Food Products Manufacturer based in Sri Lanka.

As a trusted partner, CocoRich aims to bring the unparalleled purity and exceptional taste to the Australian market, catering to the growing demand for coconut-based food products among health-conscious consumers and culinary enthusiasts.

At CocoRich, we understand the growing demand for organic and sustainable food options, and that's why we have teamed up with Sri Lanka, a trusted partner with an unwavering commitment to excellence and environmentally responsible practices.ISO 22000:2005 Food Safety Management System Certification and USDA, EU , and JAS Organic Certifications assure you that every product we offer meets the highest international standards of quality and purity.

Our collaboration with SriLanka allows us to offer a diverse range of coconut-based products, each carefully crafted to provide unparalleled taste, exceptional nutritional value, and culinary versatility. From our Coconut Flour, a gluten-free alternative for your baking needs, to our Dessicated Coconut , White Kernel Virgin Coconut Oil and Whole Kernel Virgin Coconut Oil , rich in medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) and health benefits, CocoRich has something to enhance every aspect of your culinary journey.

What we Do

The core of CocoRich business strategy is procuring premium coconuts, using cutting-edge technology in the production process, and providing a wide array of premium coconut-based goods to both domestic and foreign markets. CocoRich strives to be a market leader in pure coconut deliciousness by committing to organic excellence, sustainability, and customer pleasure. CocoRich puts the needs of its customers first ! .
